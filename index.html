What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick people into revealing passwords, bank details, or personal information through fake emails, websites, or messages.

Types of Phishing

Email Phishing

Fake emails pretending to be from banks or companies.

Smishing

Fake SMS messages asking you to click malicious links.

Vishing

Phone calls pretending to be customer support.

Fake Websites

Websites that copy real websites to steal your login details.

Safety Tips

  • ✔ Check the URL carefully.
  • ✔ Never share your OTP.
  • ✔ Enable Two-Factor Authentication.
  • ✔ Use strong passwords.
  • ✔ Avoid clicking suspicious links.
